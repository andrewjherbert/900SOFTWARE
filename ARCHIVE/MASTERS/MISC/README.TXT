Bindec
------

Load under initial instructions.

Enter at  8 to decode binary tape to 900 telecode
Enter at  9 to decode binary tape to 920 telecode
Enter at 10 to set output format via A
Enter at 11 to decode subsequent block if no self-triggered
Enter at 12 to output %

Fetch
-----

Load under initial instructions.  Enter at 8 to read input tape in 905 format 
and punch out in 900 telecode.
